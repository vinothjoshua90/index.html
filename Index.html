Questions Answered

How is NovaStore different from Gumroad or Teachable? +
NovaStore combines the best of both worlds: a curated marketplace with a rich learning platform inside a premium design. We offer AI recommendations, a 95% revenue share, instant delivery, and a community of serious creators — not just hobbyists.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept PayPal, all major credit/debit cards via Stripe, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Every transaction is secured with bank-level encryption.
How does instant delivery work? +
The moment your payment is confirmed, your purchase is added to your personal Download Library. You'll also receive an email with a secure download link. Digital files are protected and tied to your account.
Can I sell my own products on NovaStore? +
Absolutely. Creator accounts are free to open. Upload eBooks, PDFs, courses, templates, or any digital asset. We take just 5% of each sale — you keep 95%. Payouts are processed every Monday via PayPal or bank transfer, with no minimum threshold.
Is there a subscription or membership tier? +
NovaStore Pro ($19/mo) gives you unlimited downloads from a curated library of 200+ premium products, early access to new drops, AI study tools, and a 20% discount on all marketplace purchases.
What is your refund policy? +
We offer a 7-day no-questions-asked refund on all purchases under $100. For higher-ticket items, we offer a 3-day review window. Courses have a 14-day refund policy if you've completed less than 30% of the content.
